For My Bossmam        
       

To My Dearest Bossmam...

       
            In the silence where I should have stood,             I failed to be the light I could.             I spoke with clouds and words of gray,             And chased your sunshine far away.             I’m sorry for the harshness in my tone,             For leaving you to feel alone.             You're the queen, the heart, the "boss" of me,             The only place I want to be.
